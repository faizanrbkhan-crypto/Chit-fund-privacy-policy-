Privacy Policy for Chit Fund

Last updated: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our "Chit Fund" application collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we may collect the following information:

  • Group Information: Name of the kitty/chit fund group.
  • Member Details: Names, contact details, and contribution amounts added to a group.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Enable you to create, manage, and track your chit fund/kitty groups.

3. Data Security

We take security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your data. All group and member data is processed and stored securely.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (like Firebase) to support app functionality, such as database storage or crash reporting. These services have their own privacy policies.
